Who we are

MaxTech Energy is a renewable energy consulting and project development firm. We have expertise in all aspects of renewable energy consultancy and project development. Our expertise in renewable energy consultancy includes conceptual and feasibility study, detailed engineering design, and engineering project management. Our expertise in project development is the development of smart microgrid for residential, commercial/ industrial, and rural electrification.

Management Team

Munir Husein
Chairman/CEO

Munir has over a decade of experience in renewable energy design in Africa, Asia, and Latin America. He is an expert in renewable energy microgrid and specializes in solar photovoltaic and battery energy storage systems. He is the developer of MDSTool, software for designing smart microgrids. Dr. Munir obtained a doctoral degree in Electronics Engineering from Kookmin University, Seoul, South Korea. His Ph.D. dissertation is on the design and optimization of microgrid with high renewable energy penetration.


Nabila Ahmed Rufa’I
Chief Operating Officer

Nabila has nearly a decade of experience in renewable energy project planning, evaluation, and implementation. She conducted and oversaw several techno-economic analysis, energy efficiency, and energy audits programs. She is an expert in solar photovoltaic and biomass systems.
Dr. Nabila obtained a Ph.D. in Electronics and Electrical Engineering and an MSc in Electrical Engineering and Renewable Energy Systems from the University of Leeds, United Kingdom. Her Ph.D. dissertation is on the design and control of photovoltaic and biomass systems.

Board of Director

The primary responsibility of the Board of Directors of MaxTech Energy is to provide effective governance over the Firm’s affairs. These responsibilities include:

Reviewing and approving periodically long-term strategic and business plans and monitoring corporate performance against such plans;
Adopting policies of corporate conduct, including compliance with applicable laws and regulations, and periodically reviewing the adequacy of this compliance;
Evaluating the Firm’s overall risk profile and ensuring that a robust process of oversight of such risks is maintained by the Board and by executive management; and
Reviewing matters of corporate governance.
